🌱 Precautions & Care Steps

Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) has no single “cure,” but early support and lifestyle care can help individuals live fulfilling lives.

🧒 For Children

  • Enroll in early behavioral interventions (e.g., ABA therapy).
  • Provide speech and occupational therapy to build communication & daily skills.
  • Maintain structured routines to reduce stress.
  • Encourage learning through strengths and special interests.

👨‍👩‍👧 For Parents & Families

  • Join local or online autism support groups.
  • Work closely with teachers, therapists, and healthcare providers.
  • Celebrate progress, no matter how small.
  • Practice self-care to prevent caregiver burnout.

💪 Lifestyle & Daily Precautions

  • Provide a balanced diet and regular physical activity.
  • Reduce sensory overload (noise, bright lights, crowds).
  • Use calming techniques like breathing exercises or mindfulness.
  • Ensure a supportive, judgment-free home environment.
